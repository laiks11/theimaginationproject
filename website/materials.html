Core Skills Lab

Free materials, in four languages.

Every month we publish one cognitive challenge that builds a single thinking skill. Each is free, completable with paper and pen, and made to reach students regardless of geography, income, or access to technology.

One skill a month, scaffolded for independent work.

Each challenge arrives with a grade level reading and a word bank, so a student can research and reason on their own. Educators can deliver them in class with almost no preparation.
